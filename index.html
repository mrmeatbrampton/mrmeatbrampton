About US

Welcome to Mr Meat Shop !!

 We provide all kind of Fresh Marinated Chicken items, Kabab (Mild & Spicy), Fresh Ontario Goat, Ground Meat to name a few. People from across GTA have been traveling to our location to try our famous Tandoori and Curry dishes.

We slow cook these meals in a Tandoor (Traditional Clay Oven) at sizzling temperatures to make sure you get the best food. Our Fish Pakora (Fried Fish) is immensely famous and our customers come from far to try that. Do not miss our Kashimiri Fish made with our years long Recipe in Tandoor. We are committed to give you quality Food with best service. Word of mouth is the best marketing strategy we have. Our customers proudly recommend our food to friends and relatives.'
